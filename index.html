An Apology, From the Heart

Dear Nur Farisha Amira,

Thank you for all the memories, for your kindness, your patience, and for the happiness you brought into my life. I'll always be grateful for that.

Saya betul-betul minta maaf for the pain I caused you. I wish I had realised everything sooner.

I just want you to know that I miss you, and I always will have a special place for you in my heart. I hope life gives you everything you deserve, and I hope you're always happy, even if one day it's without me.

Thank you for everything, take care. ❤️
